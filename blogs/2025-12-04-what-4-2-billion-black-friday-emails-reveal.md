Bird analyzed 4.2 billion Black Friday emails to identify which messaging strategies actually work, finding that clarity beats cleverness. The most effective campaigns combined explicit Black Friday branding, specific discount percentages (50% was optimal), a single strategic emoji, time-based urgency, and mobile-optimized subject lines under 40 characters.

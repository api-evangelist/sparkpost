Most retailers send 15+ promotional messages during Cyber Weekend across email, SMS, and WhatsApp, but running each channel on a separate platform causes duplicate sends, conflicting messages, and no revenue visibility. The playbook covers coordinating cross-channel Black Friday campaigns from a single platform.
